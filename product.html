F.A.Q.

What payment methods can I use? +

You can confirm your order on WhatsApp, then pay by bank transfer or any payment option confirmed by JAGAH support.

Can I make changes to my order after it has been placed? +

Yes. Message us quickly on WhatsApp with your order number before the order is packed.

Do you offer delivery outside Lagos? +

Yes. Delivery options and fees are confirmed on WhatsApp after you send your order details.

How do I choose the right size? +

Select your usual tee size for a relaxed streetwear fit. You can also ask for sizing help before payment.

What is your return policy? +

Returns are handled through WhatsApp support. Items must be unworn and reported soon after delivery.
